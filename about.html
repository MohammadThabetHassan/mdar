Who We Are

Madar Alholool (Orbit Solutions) is a trusted consulting partner for businesses across the Middle East and North Africa. Founded over three decades ago, we have grown our expertise by delivering successful projects in multiple countries and sectors. Our mission is to drive integrated excellence—combining strategic insight, technical expertise, and local understanding.

We pride ourselves on being a regional leader with a global outlook. Our diverse team of consultants and industry experts brings together deep knowledge in engineering, finance, emerging technologies like AI, and environmental sustainability. We believe in a collaborative approach, working closely with our clients to ensure their vision is at the center of every solution we deliver.

Our Edge

  • Regional Expertise: In-depth MENA market knowledge, cultural insight, and local network connections.
  • Integrated Solutions: End-to-end consulting services that cover the full project lifecycle, from initial strategy to implementation and beyond.
  • Innovation & AI: Commitment to innovation by incorporating AI and cutting-edge technologies to enhance execution and outcomes.
  • Client-Centric Approach: A personalized approach ensuring we orbit around our clients’ vision, delivering results aligned with their goals.
